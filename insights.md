# Business Insights & Recommendations

## Finding 1: Delivery Speed Drives Satisfaction
- Fast delivery (≤7 days) scores 4.41 vs 3.06 for very slow
- Recommendation: Set seller SLAs, flag consistently late sellers

## Finding 2: Retention is the #1 Problem
- Only 3% repeat customers
- Recommendation: Post-purchase email campaigns at Day 7, 30, 90

## Finding 3: Champions Spend 2x More
- Only 782 Champions out of 93,000 customers
- Recommendation: VIP loyalty program for top customers

## Finding 4: Installments Unlock Higher Spending
- 10-installment buyers spend 4x more than full-payment buyers
- Recommendation: Promote installment options at checkout

## Finding 5: Sao Paulo Dominates Revenue
- SP generates 37% of total revenue
- Recommendation: Targeted campaigns in underserved northern states

## Finding 6: Health & Beauty Leads Revenue
- Top category at R$1.4M revenue
- Recommendation: Better packaging standards for fragile categories
